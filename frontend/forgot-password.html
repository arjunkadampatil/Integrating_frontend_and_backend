Password Reset Help
How does password reset work?
  1. Enter the email address associated with your EventSphere account
  2. Click "Send Reset Link" to receive an email with instructions
  3. Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for the reset link
  4. Click the link in the email to open the password reset page
  5. Enter your new password and confirm it
  6. You can now sign in with your new password
Important: The password reset link will expire after 1 hour for security reasons.
